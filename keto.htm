Keto Diet

Keto diets seems to be the favourite diet plans for people looking to loose fat fast. While Ketogenic deiet is a good option for a lot of people and it works just like advertised. It is a low-carb, high-fat diet, it shares a lot of similiarities with the Atkin's Diet. You have to cut down a lot on carbs which is the main source of energy for our body and replace it with fat. This puts your body in a state of ketosis.Due to this our body starts burning fat really fast fo energy.It also turn fat into ketones in the liver, which can supply energy for the brain. Ketogenic deit can cause drastic drop in blood sugar and insulin levels, which is very good for our health.

Types of Keto Diet

  • Standard ketogenic diet (SKD): This is a very low-carb, moderate-protein and high-fat diet. It typically contains 75% fat, 20% protein and only 5% carbs.
  • Cyclical ketogenic diet (CKD):This diet involves periods of higher-carb refeeds, such as 5 ketogenic days followed by 2 high-carb days.
  • Targeted ketogenic diet (TKD): This diet allows you to add carbs around workouts.
  • High-protein ketogenic diet: This is similar to a standard ketogenic diet, but includes more protein. The ratio is often 60% fat, 35% protein and 5% carbs.

Foods to Avoid

  • Starch Food: Wheat-based products, rice, pasta, cereal, etc.
  • Fruits:All sortsof fruits should be avoided.
  • Alcohol:Most beers and other alocholic drinks contain large amount of carbs that can throw you out of ketosis.
  • Processed Food: Highly processed foods contain large amount of carbohydrates.

Foods to munch on

  • Meats:Beef,lamb,pork,chicken are rich in protein and low in carbohydrates.
  • Eggs:Another great source of protein and good fats that has low carbohydrates content.
  • High Fat Dairy:Cheese,butter,yoghurt,paneer,milk.
  • Fats and oil: Coconut oil,ghee and olive oil.
